About


Our Story

Founded by Bengie and Fidel in 2011, two young entrepreneurs with passion for simplicity and tasty food. Together they established the philosophies that drive our corporate mission statement:

Focused on the Freshest high quality ingredients. Working hard every day to serve the juiciest Burgers and provide the Best service in a friendly environment for the people. Nobody does it better than Buns Burger Shop.

Buns Burger Shop prides itself on providing the Best burgers in Puerto Rico. With a simple tasty menu where you could also find: Dogs, Hand Cut Fries, Sandwiches, Shakes, Soft Drinks, Wine and Craft Beers. We take careful attention while sourcing our ingredients and that’s why we only use 100% Angus Beef, always fresh NEVER FROZEN.
